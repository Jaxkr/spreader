Welcome to spreader

Here's how it works:

  1. Click "get me something"!
  2. Observe the thing. Maybe comment on it.
  3. If you like it, press "spread" to pass it on to two people. If not, press "next".
  4. Repeat until satisfied.

  1. Upload the image you want to share to imgur or the image sharing site of your choice.
  2. Write a little caption if you want.
  3. Paste the direct think of the image into the box below.
  4. Press "submit". Your image will be spread to 2 people.
  5. Save the link to that page if you want to view stats.
